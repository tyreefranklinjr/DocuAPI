The project will analyze a few types of document:
receipts; letters; and documents (essays, articles, and etc.)

1. Receipts:

Requirements:
- Merchant Name (type: str)
- Time of Purchase (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Date of Purchase (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Subtotal (total cost; fees; and taxes): (type: float; methods: signed)
- Total (type: float; methods: signed)
- Payment Type: Cash, DISCOVER, AMEX, or Visa: (type: str)
- Payment Credentials (last 4 card number): (type: ints; methods: len: 4; methods: unsigned)

- Items Purchased (Name and Price):


Additionals:
- Transcation Number (ID): (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Location Phone: (type: int; methods: unsigned; len: 13)
- Location Email: (type: str)
- Location ID: (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Locaation Open Hours: (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Location Close Hours: (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Location Address: (type: str)
- Leadership Phone (Site Manager): (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Leadership Email (Site Manager): (type: str)
- Leadership Name (Site Manager): (type: str)
- Item Count (Eg. 'Items Sold: 3'): (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Bar Code (Transaction Number): (type: int; methods: unsigned)
- Coupons (Discount Codes): (type: str)

2. Letters

Requirements:
- Sender Name (Full Name)
- Sender's Address (Return)
- Recipient's Address
- Body

Additional:
- Recipient's Name (Business Name; Person's Name; Building Name)
- Decorators (Greeting and Closing)

3. Documnents

Requirements:
- Body

Additionals:
- Document Title
- Author
- Published Date
